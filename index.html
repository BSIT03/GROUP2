About the Automated Catfish Feeder System

The Automated Catfish Feeder System is designed to efficiently and accurately dispense fish feed at scheduled intervals. It uses components like ultrasonic sensors, Arduino microcontrollers, servo motors, and relay modules to create a reliable and energy-efficient feeding mechanism. This system helps reduce labor, ensures consistent feeding schedules, and improves the health and growth of catfish by preventing overfeeding or underfeeding.
